SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Acme Consulting LLC
("Provider") and Northwind Retail Inc. ("Client").

1. TERM
This Agreement begins on January 1, 2026 and continues for an initial period of
twelve (12) months ("Initial Term"). Unless either party provides written notice
of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days before the end of the Initial Term, this
Agreement will automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods
("Auto-Renewal Clause").

2. SERVICES
Provider will deliver monthly IT infrastructure consulting, including system audits,
security reviews, and on-call technical support during business hours (9am-6pm, Mon-Fri).

3. FEES
Client agrees to pay Provider a flat monthly fee of $8,500, invoiced on the first
business day of each month. Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month.

4. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach if the breach is not
cured within fifteen (15) days of written notice. Client may terminate for convenience
with sixty (60) days' written notice, subject to a early-termination fee equal to
two (2) months of fees.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to keep confidential information disclosed during the engagement
private for a period of three (3) years following termination of this Agreement.

6. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid
by Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
